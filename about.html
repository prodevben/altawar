About Al Tawar Solutions

Innovation, Quality, and Excellence in Water Cooling Technology

Our Story

Al Tawar Refrigerators & A/C Factory LLC established in 2011 and based in Sharjah, UAE, has been a trusted leader in manufacturing of stainless steel water coolers, glass door refrigerator and water tank chillers. Serving industrial and commercial sectors across the UAE, Middle East, and African countries, we bring decades of expertise to every product we create

Our commitment to excellence is evident in each stage of our production. We use premium raw materials, innovative processes, and precision craftsmanship, supported by stringent quality assurance procedures, to deliver outstanding durability and performance. Our products are certified to the highest standards, including CB, ROHS, ESMA.

We exceed expectations by earning trust through reliability, integrity, and ethics, building lasting partnerships while driving opportunities.
